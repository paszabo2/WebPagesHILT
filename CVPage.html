Librarian Experience

Music & Public Service Librarian: Ohio Wesleyan University

  • Manages the Kinnison Music Library (approximately 15,000 items, including books, scores, CDs, DVDs, vinyl recordings, and journals).
  • Supervises 10-12 student assistants.
  • Supervises the circulation of music library materials.
  • Participates in collection development: books, musical scores, sound recordings, and electronic resources.
  • Teaches information literacy sessions: OWU’s discovery layer and online library catalog, research on the open Web, use of specific resources, research strategies.
  • Provides 6-8 hours per week of general reference service at the main library.
  • Participates in technical services: retrospective conversion of OWU’s Vinyl collection, processing of new sound recordings, binding of scores with parts.

Academic Service

Culture Cafe

  • A student centered activity that seeks to create connections with the OWU student community by expanding the library’s role as a place where discovery thrives. Culture Café is related to the evolving concept of ‘library as place’ in which the library experience expands beyond its traditional boundaries. In this case, we push on those boundaries in the spirit of intellectual curiosity. Culture Café features performances of a wide variety, including music, poetry, and dance. It is held at least once per semester from 8-9pm on a Thursday evening in the main library's cafe.
